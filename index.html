About Me

I am a first-year Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science at Yale University. I am working on distributed systems with Prof. Mahesh Balakrishnan.

I have worked on in-memory computing engine HANA, which leverages multi-core, large-scale main memory to deal with (at least) terabyte-scale data in a single machine.

I received B.S. in Electrical and Computer Engineering at Seoul National University with honors.
